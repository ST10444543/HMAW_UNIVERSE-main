Luna

The Guardian of the Night

Biography

Luna, the Guardian of the Night, draws her power from the moon, wielding its energy to protect the world from dark forces. With her mystical abilities, she illuminates the shadows and brings calm in the chaos.

Powers and Abilities

  • Moonlight Manipulation: Can control and project moonlight to create illusions and shield allies.
  • Enhanced Agility: Possesses superhuman agility and reflexes, allowing her to navigate through darkness effortlessly.
  • Empathy with Night Creatures: Can communicate and summon nocturnal creatures to aid her in battle.

Affiliations

Luna stands united with fellow guardians, defending against threats to the realm of light and hope.
